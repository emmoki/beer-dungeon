Mage

    Generally, [Mages] have much more difficulty leveling than other classes. While a [Warrior] must simply challenge ever-greater foes in order to level, even Archmages of Wistram have difficulty leveling as [Mages] must not only study, but practice magic of great power. In the current age, lack of a centralized education across nations have led to variations of [Mage] classes including [Hedge Wizards], [Witch Doctors], [Spellswords], etc..[Mages] seem to be more resistant to several kinds of magic, and can either shrug off certain spells or recover quicker than non-Mage classes - this holds especially true for mind-affecting spells and possibly seeing through illusions. Physical attacks however need to be warded off, even when caused by magical objects.
